## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Arena detail SHALL support interactive drill-down
Selecting a leaderboard group or applying a leaderboard filter SHALL update a
detailed panel for that exact race scope, including ranking list and summary
metrics, without leaving the page.

#### Scenario: Visitor drills into one leaderboard group
- **WHEN** a visitor selects one leaderboard group or changes a ranking filter
- **THEN** the detail panel updates to the resulting ranking scope without a
  page transition
